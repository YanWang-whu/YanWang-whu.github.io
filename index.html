Selected Research

  • Identified that loss of INS-1 neuropeptide reverses ethanol avoidance behavior under high-salt conditions.
  • Construct neuron-specific knockout/rescue ins-1 worm strains and conduct behavioral analysis.
  • Perform neuronal calcium imaging analysis to investigate the effect of ins-1 on neural circuit function.
  • Identified ASEL as an important target neuron of INS-1 through my research.
  • Conduct imaging, image processing, and data analysis.
  • The POST-IT system developed in the laboratory uses PafA ligase to label small drug target proteins.
  • Use yeast display technology with random mutation to obtain a PafA mutant library.
  • Obtain highly performing mutants through FACS.
  • Initially obtained a mutant that significantly reduced the required labeling time and substrate concentration.
  • Evaluate the efficacy of PafA mutants in recognizing small-molecule targets.
  • The core concepts, prototype design, and technical roadmap were proposed by me.
  • Construct colony-level logic gates using spatial diffusion together with high-pass and band-pass circuits.
  • Build a serial addition calculator using selected orthogonal quorum-sensing systems.
  • Separate the functional domain of the degradation enzyme and introduce a light-controlled degradation tag.
